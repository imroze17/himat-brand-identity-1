## Idea A — Architectural Minimalism

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets Contemporary Real Estate Luxury

**Core Principles:**
- Extreme precision in layout with strict grid alignment
- Typography as the primary visual element
- Negative space used as a luxury signal
- Monochromatic base with a single warm accent

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Pure Black (#0A0A0A) — authority, permanence
- Secondary: Warm White (#F7F5F2) — clean, premium paper feel
- Accent: Deep Champagne Gold (#C9A84C) — real estate prestige, warmth
- Neutral: Warm Gray (#8A8580) — supporting text, dividers

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric full-bleed sections alternating between black and white backgrounds
- Logo lockup section with generous whitespace
- Color swatch blocks displayed as large full-width strips
- Typography specimen shown at massive scale
- Brand applications in realistic mockup photography style

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule lines as section dividers
- Large oversized numerals as section labels (01, 02, 03...)
- The geometric logo pattern used as a repeating background texture

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section fade-ins
- Subtle parallax on hero section
- Hover states reveal brand color fills

**Animation:**
- Sections fade up on scroll entry
- Logo elements animate in sequentially on page load
- Color swatches slide in from left

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — for brand name, hero statements
- Body: DM Sans (geometric sans) — for descriptions, contact info
- Accent: DM Mono — for labels, hex codes, technical details

## Idea B — Editorial Brutalism

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Editorial Design meets Brutalist Web Aesthetics

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, unpolished grid with intentional asymmetry
- Bold typographic contrasts (massive vs. tiny)
- Photography bleeds to edges with no padding
- Structural honesty — no decorative flourishes

**Color Philosophy:**
- Black (#000000) — dominant, commanding
- Off-white (#F0EDE8) — warm paper texture feel
- Signal Red (#C0392B) — single accent for emphasis moments
- No gradients, no shadows

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Newspaper-style multi-column layouts
- Text overflows and bleeds into image areas
- Sections divided by thick black rules
- Logo displayed at extreme scale variations

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized pull quotes in bold serif
- Grid lines visible as design elements
- Rotated text labels on section sides

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Abrupt, immediate transitions
- No easing — snap to position
- Bold hover color inversions

**Animation:**
- Text slides in from edges
- Sections snap into view on scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: Bebas Neue — massive, condensed
- Body: IBM Plex Sans — structured, editorial
- Labels: IBM Plex Mono — technical precision

## Idea C — Refined Modernism (SELECTED)

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Brand Identity Presentation — Behance Portfolio Style

**Core Principles:**
- Sophisticated restraint: every element earns its place
- Alternating dark/light sections create visual rhythm
- Brand applications shown in realistic, aspirational contexts
- Typography hierarchy is clear and purposeful

**Color Philosophy:**
- Ink Black (#0D0D0D) — primary brand color, from the logo
- Warm White (#FAFAF8) — primary background, premium feel
- Slate Navy (#1C2B3A) — deep accent for dark sections, real estate gravitas
- Champagne Gold (#B8963E) — prestige accent, real estate luxury signal
- Stone Gray (#6B7280) — supporting text, captions

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width sections with generous vertical rhythm
- Alternating black/white/navy backgrounds for visual interest
- Logo showcase in three colorways (white bg, black bg, gold bg)
- Color palette as large horizontal swatches with labels
- Typography specimen section with alphabet display
- Brand application mockups: business card, signage, social media

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal rules as section separators
- The 3×3 diamond logo grid used as a decorative pattern at low opacity
- Section numbers in small caps with gold accent

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations
- Elegant fade-and-rise entrance for each section
- Subtle scale transforms on hover for mockup cards

**Animation:**
- Hero: Logo fades in, then text slides up
- Sections: Staggered fade-up on scroll intersection
- Color swatches: Sequential slide-in from left
- Mockups: Gentle lift on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif) — for hero statements, brand name display
- UI/Body: Outfit (geometric sans) — for all body text, labels, contact info
- Mono: JetBrains Mono — for hex codes, technical labels
